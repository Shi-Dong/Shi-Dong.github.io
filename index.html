Shi Dong (董仕)

 

Research Scientist, Google DeepMind

Abstract

I received my Ph.D. degree from Stanford University, and my doctoral research, advised by Prof. Benjamin Van Roy, focuses on developing a more flexible framework to study reinforcement learning. Motivated by the passion for natural languages, I am now dedicated to understanding and unleashing the power of reinforcement learning in large language models (LLMs). Prior to joining Google DeepMind, I was a Senior Researcher at Knowledge and Language Team, Microsoft, where I had the fortune to work with Chenguang Zhu. Beyond research, I am also a zealous fan of British and European history.
